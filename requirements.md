# Missionary Information Website

## Project Overview

The goal of this project is to create a professional, visually engaging, and easy-to-navigate informational website for a Canadian missionary who served in Peru. The website will primarily showcase the missionary's story, ministry work, experiences, photographs, and impact through a clean and modern design.

The website should feel personal, trustworthy, and inspiring while remaining simple for visitors of all ages to navigate.

---

# Design Requirements

## Visual Style

* Clean and modern layout
* Christian and mission-focused aesthetic
* Warm and welcoming color palette
* Professional typography
* Strong emphasis on storytelling and photography
* Mobile-first responsive design
* Accessibility compliant (WCAG best practices)

## Inspiration

The design should resemble a modern nonprofit or ministry website rather than a corporate website.

Key themes:

* Faith
* Service
* Community
* Missions
* Peru culture and landscapes

Use high-quality imagery throughout the site.

---

# Functional Requirements

## 1. Homepage

### Hero Section

Display:

* Missionary name
* Professional hero image
* Short mission statement
* Call-to-action buttons:

  * Learn More
  * View Gallery

### Introduction Section

Brief overview including:

* Who the missionary is
* Where they served
* Years of service
* Short summary of ministry work

### Featured Highlights

Display key accomplishments or ministry highlights:

* Churches supported
* Communities served
* Outreach programs
* Years in Peru

Present these in visually appealing cards.

---

## 2. About Section

Dedicated page or section describing:

### Biography

* Background in Canada
* Faith journey
* Calling to missions
* Preparation and training

### Mission Work

* Why Peru was chosen
* Ministry goals
* Community involvement
* Challenges and successes

Include supporting images alongside text.

---

## 3. Peru Ministry Section

A detailed section focused on the work completed in Peru.

Content may include:

* Community outreach
* Church planting
* Teaching and discipleship
* Humanitarian work
* Youth programs
* Local partnerships

Features:

* Text content blocks
* Image galleries
* Timeline of ministry activities

---

## 4. Photo Gallery

Dedicated gallery page.

Features:

* Responsive image grid
* Lightbox image viewing
* Image captions
* Lazy loading for performance

Categories may include:

* Ministry Work
* Communities
* Churches
* Events
* Peru Landscapes

---

## 5. Testimonies & Impact

Display stories and testimonies.

Possible content:

* Community impact stories
* Personal reflections
* Ministry milestones
* Quotes from local partners

Layout:

* Testimonial cards
* Featured quotes
* Highlight sections

---

## 6. Timeline of Service

Interactive or visual timeline showing:

* Early life in Canada
* Calling to missions
* Arrival in Peru
* Major ministry milestones
* Current activities

---

## 7. Support & Contact Section

### Contact Information

Display:

* Email address
* Contact form

Form fields:

* Name
* Email
* Message

### Optional Support Information

If desired:

* Donation information
* Ministry organization links
* Prayer request information

# Technical Requirements

## Frontend

* Next.js
* React
* TypeScript
* Tailwind CSS

## Performance

Target:

* Lighthouse score 90+
* Optimized images
* Fast page loading
* Responsive design

## SEO

Implement:

* Meta titles
* Meta descriptions
* Open Graph tags
* Structured data where applicable

---

# Content Management

Content should be easily editable.

Recommended options:

* Markdown files
* JSON content files
* Headless CMS (optional)

All text and images should be replaceable without modifying major code structures.

---

# Responsive Design

Support:

* Mobile phones
* Tablets
* Laptops
* Desktop monitors

The experience should remain visually appealing across all screen sizes.

---

# Accessibility

Implement:

* Proper heading hierarchy
* Keyboard navigation support
* Image alt text
* Sufficient color contrast
* Screen-reader friendly components

---

# Deliverables

The completed website should include:

* Homepage
* About page
* Peru Ministry page
* Photo Gallery
* Timeline section
* Contact page
* Fully responsive design
* SEO optimization
* Optimized images and performance

The final result should communicate the missionary's story in a compelling, respectful, and visually engaging way while highlighting their ministry work and impact in Peru.
